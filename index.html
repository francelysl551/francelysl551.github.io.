No one should go through this alone

Immigration is a growing issue in the United States, each day the number of refugees is increasing.
Families suffer and are being separated from each other, we aim to inform others about these injustices.
Project Take Action: Immigration raises awareness and informs others about immigration in hopes to bring radical change.
This website calls out society to advocate and suggest ways to take action.

Did You Know?

Immigrants today are putting down roots across the United States, in contrast to trends seen 50 years ago

As immigrants and their descendants integrate into American society, many aspects of their lives improve.

High-skilled immigration increases innovation.

Economy is higher and grows faster with more immigrants

Undocumented immigrants are in no way criminals or bad people.
